## Decision rule

- **Core buy:** near/below historical avg multiple (2) + dividend intact (3) +
  RRR ≥ 1.0 (4) + still net cash (5). Quality bought at a fair price.
- **Wait for pullback:** thesis intact but multiple stretched (2 fails alone).
  The quality does not go away — let valuation come to you.
- **Pass:** RRR < 1.0 (4) OR dividend cut to fund capex (3) OR balance sheet
  levering (5). The quality thesis is breaking.

## Bull case
- Cleanest cash conversion in E&P (low circular-debt drag → profits become dividends).
- Repeatable exploration success; Shams-1 validates the growth pivot.
- Historically net cash — funds both growth capex and dividend.
- Gas-weighted → insulated from falling-oil tape (unlike OGDC/PPL).
- Rate-cut cycle compresses the discount rate on stable cashflows.

## Bear case
- Guaranteed-return cushion fading → earnings more gas-price/FX-sensitive than the record suggests.
- Reserve depletion risk on the mature core Mari (Daharki) field.
- Capital-allocation risk in the diversification pivot (spending the cash hoard).
- Circular debt lower than peers, not zero — can build if customer mix shifts.
- Policy risk: gas pricing, petroleum levy, windfall taxes can hit E&P economics overnight.
